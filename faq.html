Frequently Asked Questions

Q) What do the cakes cost?

There are no set prices on cakes outside of seasonal offerings (ie. Christmas, Valentine's Days), as the price varies greatly from cake to cake depending on ingredients, people to feed and detail. Consultations allow me to offer a variety of options catered to your wants and budget.

Q) How do I place an order?

Simply use the "Contact" section of the website to send me an email and I will contact you to discuss your ideas. While seasonal offerings are on you can use this feature to book directly. On both occasions please be mindful of the "Calander" feature on the website. When it indicates I am "fully booked", I am truly fully booked and cannot facilitate an order at this time.

Q) How far in advance should I book?

I would recommend contacting me no later than one month in advance for smaller cakes, as there is limited availabilty and spaces tend to fill up fast. For larger cakes, I would recommend contacting me as soon as possible so that I can start work on your design and book you in to avoid disappointment.

Q) Is delivery available?

Delivery is available on a case by case basis, if I can facilitate it I can deliver straight to your door. I am a one woman operation so occasionally I may not have the time, but where I can I will provide free delivery to your door/venue.
